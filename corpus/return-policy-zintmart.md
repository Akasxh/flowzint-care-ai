# ZintMart Return & Refund Policy

**Effective:** 1 January 2026  
**Brand:** ZintMart — Indian D2C electronics & fashion  
**Support email:** care@zintmart.in

---

## Return windows by category

| Category | Return window | Condition |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| **Electronics** (phones, earbuds, chargers, smartwatches) | **7 days** from delivery | Unused, original packaging, all accessories |
| **Fashion** (kurtas, sarees, ethnic wear, footwear) | **15 days** from delivery | Unworn, tags attached, no stains or odour |
| **Home & kitchen** (small appliances under ₹5,000) | **7 days** | Unused, box intact |
| **Beauty & personal care** | **Non-returnable** (hygiene) | — |

---

## Non-returnable items

- Opened skincare, cosmetics, or personal hygiene products
- Customised or monogrammed fashion items
- Digital gift cards and warranty-only SKUs
- Items marked **Final Sale** at checkout
- Products with missing IMEI/serial when required for electronics

---

## Return methods

### 1. Free doorstep pickup (metro cities)

Available in **Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad**.

- Schedule pickup in My Orders → Return Item
- Keep product in original box; affix return label if provided
- Pickup within **2–3 business days** of approval

### 2. Self-ship (tier-2/3 cities)

- Ship to: ZintMart Returns, Plot 14, Sector 62, Noida, UP 201301
- Use trackable courier; retain receipt until refund
- **₹100 shipping credit** added to refund if return is approved for seller fault (wrong/damaged item)

---

## Refund timeline

| Payment method | Refund credit |
|----------------|---------------|
| UPI / Net banking / Credit card | **5–7 business days** after warehouse QC pass |
| COD orders | Refund to **ZintMart Wallet** or bank account (NEFT) within **7–10 business days** |
| EMI / Pay Later | Refund to original lender per their cycle; may take **10–14 business days** |

Refunds are processed only after quality check at our Noida returns centre.

---

## Exchanges

- **Wrong size (fashion):** One free exchange within 15 days if stock available
- **Defective electronics:** Replace or full refund within 7 days; no restocking fee
- Exchange shipping is **free** for ZintMart error (wrong item, damage in transit)

---

## Damaged or wrong item received

1. Report within **48 hours** of delivery with photos (outer box + product + shipping label)
2. Provide **order ID** (format `FZ-####`)
3. Do not discard packaging until case is closed
4. Priority pickup arranged for verified damage claims
